## Notes

- Characteristics (Might, Agility, etc.) are extremely high frequency but also
  common English words. These would need careful tiering (dangerous or contextual
  with smart context detection).
- Edge/Bane are similarly dual-use (game term vs common English).
- Combat action types (Main Action, Move Action, Triggered Action) are unambiguous
  game terms and would be safe to auto-link.
- Encounter types (Montage Test, Respite, Negotiation) are moderately ambiguous
  but appear in clearly mechanical contexts most of the time.
